Trust & Compliance

Sirio Data Labs

Last updated: March 2026

1. Our Commitment

At Sirio Data Labs, trust, transparency, and responsible data practices are foundational to how we design and operate our data products.

We are committed to building reliable, secure, and clear data services that meet the expectations of professional and enterprise customers, while continuously improving our organizational and technical practices.

2. Data Ethics and Responsibility

Sirio Data Labs is committed to responsible data use and ethical data handling.

  • We do not knowingly collect, process, or distribute personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Our datasets are based on aggregated, processed, and non‑personal data.
  • Data products are designed for analytical and decision‑support purposes, not for surveillance or individual profiling.

3. Security Awareness

Security is treated as an ongoing operational responsibility.

  • We apply industry‑aligned security practices appropriate to a modern cloud‑based data provider.
  • Infrastructure is hosted using reputable cloud platforms with established security controls.
  • Access to internal systems and datasets is restricted based on operational necessity.

More details are available in our security.html>Security & Data Handling documentation.

4. Compliance Mindset

As an early‑stage organization, Sirio Data Labs follows a compliance‑first mindset:

  • Clear Terms of Service govern product usage
  • A transparent Privacy Policy explains how information is handled
  • Defined Support processes establish expectations by product tier
  • Documentation is continuously improved as the organization evolves

Formal certifications (such as ISO or SOC) may be evaluated as the organization scales and business needs justify them.

5. Transparency and Accountability

We believe trust is built through clarity and accountability.

  • Product scope, limitations, and intended use are clearly communicated
  • Support channels are openly available
  • A designated organizational lead is responsible for oversight and communication

Questions related to trust, compliance, or responsible data practices are welcomed.

6. Continuous Improvement

Sirio Data Labs views trust and compliance as evolving processes.

We regularly review our:

  • Operational practices
  • Data handling workflows
  • Security posture
  • Customer feedback

Improvements are incorporated as part of normal product and organizational development.
