1. Introduction

Sirio Data Labs (“Sirio Data Labs”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy and security of information related to users of our website, datasets, and data products (collectively, the “Services”).

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when you access or use our Services.

2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Visitors of the Sirio Data Labs website
  • Users of our data products and datasets
  • Communications with Sirio Data Labs through electronic means

This policy does not apply to third‑party services, platforms, or websites that may be linked from our Services.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Non‑Personal Information

We may collect limited non‑personal and technical information, such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Approximate location (country or region)
  • Access timestamps
  • Pages accessed on our website

This information is used solely for operational, analytical, and security purposes.

3.2 Personal Information

Sirio Data Labs does not intentionally collect or process personal data related to individuals contained within the datasets we provide.

Personal information may be collected only when voluntarily provided, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Organization name
  • Communication content

This typically occurs when contacting us for support, inquiries, or business communication.

4. Use of Information

Collected information may be used to:

  • Operate and maintain the Services
  • Provide customer support
  • Communicate regarding services and updates
  • Improve data quality, performance, and reliability
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

Sirio Data Labs does not sell personal information to third parties.

5. Data Products and Content

Our data products consist of aggregated, processed, and non‑personal datasets derived from reliable public or licensed sources.

Sirio Data Labs does not process, store, or distribute personally identifiable information (PII) as part of its datasets.

6. Data Storage and Security

Sirio Data Labs applies reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

Data is hosted using reputable cloud infrastructure providers that implement industry‑standard physical and logical security controls.

However, no method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce agreements

8. Cookies and Tracking

Sirio Data Labs may use basic cookies or similar technologies to support website functionality and collect aggregated usage information.

We do not use cookies for advertising or behavioral tracking purposes.

Our Services may integrate with or link to third‑party platforms (such as cloud service providers). Sirio Data Labs is not responsible for the privacy practices of those third‑party services.

We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third‑party services they interact with.

10. International Users

Sirio Data Labs operates from Costa Rica. By accessing or using the Services from another jurisdiction, you acknowledge that information may be processed in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable local laws.
